PAULO MOURA
INTERACTIVE DESIGNER/ART DIRECTOR

He started his career as Designer in 2012 when he found the Studio Digital Point in Estremoz after finished
a Multimedia Course in EPRAL. With great passion for Surrealism and Street Art he have a outgoing
personality and proactive mindset always looking for knowing more.
